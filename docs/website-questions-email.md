# Email Draft -- IntermosCraft Website Kickoff

---

**Subject:** IntermosCraft Website -- A Few Questions Before We Start

---

Hi [Name],

I'm getting ready to start working on the IntermosCraft website. Before I dive in, I need to understand how you see it so I can build something that truly fits the brand.

I'll keep it short -- just a few key questions:

---

### The Look & Feel

1. **Can you send me 2-3 websites you like?** (doesn't have to be furniture -- could be any brand). Just tell me briefly what you like about each one. This helps me more than anything else.

2. **What should someone feel when they open the site?** For example: exclusive high-end showroom? Warm artisan workshop? Clean and minimal? Bold and dramatic?

### The Structure

3. **Beyond products, what else should be on the site?** For example:
   - Your story / about the brand
   - A portfolio of past projects (custom pieces, installations)
   - A way for customers to request custom/bespoke orders
   - A blog or journal
   - Something else?

4. **How important is the "custom order" side of things?** Should there be a dedicated section where people can describe what they want, upload inspiration photos, request a quote?

### The Products & Shop

5. **Roughly how many products will be in the shop at launch?**

6. **Are the products fixed, or can customers customize them?** (e.g., choose wood type, metal finish, size)

7. **Should all products have a price and "Add to Cart," or should some (or all) be "Request a Quote" instead?**

8. **Where do you ship?** CZ + SK only, or wider?

### Content

9. **Do you have professional photos of your products and workshop ready?** (or are you planning a photoshoot?)

10. **Do you have any text/copy for the site, or should that be written from scratch?**

### Quick Practical Ones

11. **Do you have a domain name already?** (e.g., intermoscraft.com)

12. **Is there a target launch date you have in mind?**

13. **What's more important to launch first -- the brand presentation (homepage, about, portfolio) or the online shop?** We can do both together, but good to know your priority.

---

That's it for now. Don't overthink the answers -- even short replies help me get started. I'll have a few follow-up questions once I see your answers, but this is enough to kick things off.

Looking forward to it!

[Your name]
